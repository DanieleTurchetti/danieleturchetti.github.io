Past events

From 2012 to 2014, together with Thibaud Lemanissier, I have organized the reading group “Les Incontournables” in Jussieu, where we read a series of fundamental papers in algebraic geometry.

In November 2017, together with Jérôme Poineau, I have organized the ''Algebraic Geometry with `Fancy’ coefficients'' conference. A program is still available here.

In January 2020, together with Susan Cooper, Sara Faridi, and Mayada Shahada, I have organized the "Combinatorial Algebra meets Algebraic Combinatorics 2020” conference. The webpage of the conference is here.
