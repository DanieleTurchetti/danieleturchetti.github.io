Outreach

- Images des Mathématiques
- Association Paris Montagne 
- Fête de la Science
- Equipe de diffusion de la Culture Mathématique de l'Université de Caen
- UTL - Essonne 
- IMAGINARY
- Girl Guides of Canada
- WISE Atlantic 
- Engineers NS
- Discovery Centre Halifax 
- Let’s Talk Science
- Atlantic Science Link Association (ASLA)
- SuperNOVA
- BRIDGES organization

and I am always looking for new ways to spread the message that mathematics is useful, empowering, and beautiful.
