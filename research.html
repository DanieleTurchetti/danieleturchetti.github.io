Research

My current research revolves around non-archimedean geometry, tropical geometry, and Galois theory, with a special focus on the arithmetic of curves, abelian varieties, and their moduli spaces.

More broadly, I am interested in a variety of topics where algebra, arithmetic, and geometry come together in surprising ways, and in how to apply these to other branches of science. Lately, I have been thinking about computational algebraic geometry, topological data analysis, and algebraic statistics.
